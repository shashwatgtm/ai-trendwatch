üìã Detailed Strategy Breakdown

1. Skip the Education Phase

With 86% already using AI and 39% understanding it deeply, don't spend cycles explaining what AI is. Start with what it does for their specific context.

2. Lead with Transformation, Not Reassurance

In Western markets, you often need to address fears before presenting benefits. In India, 69% are already optimistic. Lead with ambitious outcomes.

3. Test Bold Positioning Here First

India's informed optimism reduces the risk of aggressive messaging. If it resonates here, it likely has global potential.

4. Leverage Data-Sharing Willingness

72% will share data for better products. Build India-specific feedback loops and use India as your product development laboratory.

5. Shorten Your Sales Cycles

Trust exists. Understanding exists. Skip the lengthy education phases that Western enterprise sales require.
