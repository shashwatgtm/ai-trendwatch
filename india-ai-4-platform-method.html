  • ‚Ä¢ Self-reported AI usage, knowledge, and attitudes
  • ‚Ä¢ Trust in AI accuracy, workplace impact, learning intent
  • ‚Ä¢ Sentiment (promising vs concerning scales)
  • ‚Ä¢ Cross-country comparison (21 countries)

Data Parameters

  • ‚Ä¢ Sample: ~21,000 adults (18+), ~1,000 per country
  • ‚Ä¢ Countries: 21 (including India)
  • ‚Ä¢ Period: Sep 22 ‚Äì Oct 10, 2025
  • ‚Ä¢ Published: January 15, 2026

‚ö†Ô∏è Caveats: Online sampling methodology inherently skews towards urban, educated, digitally connected respondents ‚Äî especially significant in India where 500M+ lack regular internet access. ~1,000 respondents cannot represent 1.4 billion people. Self-reported usage often over-estimates actual behaviour. Google-sponsored survey may have framing bias towards positive AI narratives.
