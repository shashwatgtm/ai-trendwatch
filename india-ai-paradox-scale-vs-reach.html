üìê Methodology: 4-Source Triangulation

Anthropic Economic Index

~58,000 Claude.ai conversations from India (Nov 2025). Tasks mapped to O*NET occupation codes. Measures actual usage patterns. Limitation: Claude's user base skews technical and English-speaking.

Published: Feb 16, 2026

OpenAI Signals

ChatGPT platform data from India's 100M+ WAU base. Released at India AI Impact Summit alongside commercial announcements. Data real but selectively presented ‚Äî no per-capita data.

Published: Feb 20, 2026

Google/Ipsos Survey

Online survey, ~21,000 adults (18+), 21 countries, Sep 22‚ÄìOct 10, 2025. ~1,000 Indian respondents. Online sampling skews urban and digitally connected ‚Äî caveat for a country where 500M+ lack regular internet.

Published: Jan 15, 2026

Microsoft AI Diffusion

Aggregated, anonymised Microsoft telemetry adjusted for OS/device market share, internet penetration, and population. Most methodologically rigorous for cross-country comparison. Not limited to one product.

Published: Jan 8, 2026
